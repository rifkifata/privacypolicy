Privacy Policy for Gold Notification

Last updated: 14 April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Gold Notification ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our application "Gold Notification" (the "App"). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our service, we collect a limited amount of non-personally identifiable information:

  • Anonymous Installation ID (UUID): Upon first launch, the App generates a random, anonymous identifier (UUID) which is stored on your device. This ID is used solely to manage your price alerts on our server and is not linked to your identity. We do not collect hardware-based device IDs (like ANDROID_ID or IMEI).
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) Token: To send you the price alert notifications you set up, we collect and store your FCM registration token. This token is provided by Google and is necessary for the push notification functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To register your device on our server so you can create and receive price alerts.
  • To send push notifications when a price target you have set is reached.
  • To troubleshoot and improve the functionality of our App.

4. Data Source and Disclaimer

The financial data, including gold prices and currency exchange rates, displayed in the App is obtained through our backend system. Our system aggregates and processes this data from various reputable and publicly available global financial data providers. We strive for accuracy, but we do not guarantee the timeliness or correctness of the data. All information provided by the App is for informational purposes only and should not be considered as financial advice.

5. Third-Party Services

Our App utilizes the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data (such as IP address, device identifiers, and ad interaction data) to provide personalized advertising. You can learn more about how Google handles data in its advertising policies.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): We use FCM to deliver push notifications. This service is part of the Google Firebase platform. Please refer to Firebase's privacy policy for more details.

6. Permissions

The Gold Notification app requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • INTERNET: To fetch price data from our servers and to load advertisements.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: (On Android 13 and above) To display price alert notifications on your device.

7. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. The Anonymous Installation ID and FCM Token are transmitted securely over HTTPS to our servers. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with information, please contact us so that we can take necessary action.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
