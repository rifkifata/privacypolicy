Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08 April 2026

App name: Gold Notification

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how we handle user information in our app “Gold Notification”. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This application provides real-time gold price tracking and push notification alerts.

2. Information We Collect

To provide the core functionality of the app, we collect the following anonymous data:

  • Device ID & FCM Token: When you use the Price Alert feature, we collect an anonymous Device Identifier and a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token. This is strictly used to send push notifications to your device when the gold price reaches your target.
  • Alert Preferences: We store the target prices and conditions (e.g., price goes UP or DOWN) that you set within the app securely on our servers.

We do not collect, store, or require any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location.

3. Third-Party Services & Advertising

Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain non-personal data (such as Advertising ID, device type, IP address, and ad interaction data) to serve relevant ads and evaluate ad performance.
  • Google Firebase: We use Firebase to deliver push notifications securely to your device.

For more information, please refer to Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Financial Data Disclaimer

The gold price data displayed in this application is aggregated from publicly available global financial markets and third-party public sources. The data is provided "as is" strictly for general informational and educational purposes.

We do not guarantee the 100% accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of the data. The information provided in this app does not constitute financial, investment, or trading advice. The developer is not responsible or liable for any financial decisions or trading losses made based on the information provided in this app.

5. Data Security & Retention

Your alert preferences and anonymous Device ID are stored securely on our servers. If you delete an alert within the app, the data is permanently removed from our active database. Furthermore, if you uninstall the application, your FCM token becomes invalid, and our system will automatically clean up and remove your device data from our servers.

6. Permissions

This app requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Internet Access: To fetch the latest gold prices and load advertisements.
  • Notifications (Android 13+): To send you push notifications when your price alerts are triggered.

7. Children’s Privacy

Because this app deals with financial market data, it is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this document. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
