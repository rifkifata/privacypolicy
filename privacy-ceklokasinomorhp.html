Important: Clarifying "Lacak" (Track) vs. HLR Lookup

   

Our application's name uses the word "Lacak" (which translates to "Track") to reach a broader audience. However, it is crucial to understand that this Application is **NOT A REAL-TIME GPS LOCATION TRACKER**. Its core function is **HLR (Home Location Register) Lookup**.

   

What is HLR? You can think of HLR as the "birthplace" of a SIM card number. Regardless of where the phone is physically located, its HLR information will always point to the region where the card was first activated or registered. For example, even if a phone number registered in Jakarta is currently in another city, an HLR search will always show that it is a Jakarta-based number.

   

This Application **CANNOT** and **WILL NEVER** be able to determine the real-time physical location of a user or the name of the number's owner. Such data is confidential and legally protected by mobile operators.

   

Data Source and Accuracy

   

The provider and regional information displayed in this Application is based on a public database (data.json) that we compiled from various sources on the internet. While we strive to present accurate data, we **do not guarantee 100% accuracy or timeliness** of this information. Phone numbers can be ported to different operators, or there may be data changes from providers that are not yet reflected in our database.

   

The information provided is for educational and referential purposes only. Do not use information from this Application for any official or legal purposes.
