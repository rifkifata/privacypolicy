Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

App name: MyQibla: Compass & Tasbih

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how we handle user information in our app "MyQibla: Compass & Tasbih". We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This app operates 100% offline, is entirely ad-free, and does not collect or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email, phone number, or device ID. All functions of the app operate locally on your device.

3. Location Data

To provide accurate Qibla direction, this app requires access to your device's Location data (GPS). Please note: Your location data is only processed locally on your device to calculate the angle of the Kaaba. We do not store your location data, nor do we transmit or share it with any third parties or servers.

4. Third-Party Services & Ads

Unlike many other apps, MyQibla is completely free of advertisements. We do not use third-party ad networks (such as AdMob), analytics tools, or tracking software. Your usage remains completely private.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for general audiences aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Since the app operates entirely offline and does not collect data, children's privacy is naturally protected.

6. Permissions

To function properly, this app requests the following device permissions:

  • Location: Used exclusively to calculate the Qibla direction relative to your current geographical position.
  • Vibration (Haptic Feedback): Used to provide a tactile response when using the digital Tasbih and when the correct Qibla direction is found.

This app does not request or require Internet access, camera access, or storage access.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
