Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 August 2025

App name: Kode Paket Murah All Operator

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the “Kode Paket Murah All Operator” app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This app is designed for general audiences (13+) and does not collect personal data from users.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as name, email, phone number, precise location, or device ID. We do not run analytics.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain non-personal data (such as device information, approximate location, advertising identifiers, and ad interaction data) to provide and improve advertising services in accordance with Google’s policies.

Please refer to Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sources & Updates

The app loads a read-only JSON data file containing **public USSD references** from a GitHub Gist: kode_paket.json . All data is **collected and managed by the app developer** from public sources on the internet. The file may be updated from time to time to add or modify the list of codes. No user data is sent back to our servers.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for users aged 13 and above and is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not store user data on our servers. Ad-related data is handled securely by Google and its partners according to their policies.

7. Permissions

  • INTERNET – required to fetch the JSON data and to load ads.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID – used by Google advertising services (AdMob) for ad personalization/measurement as per Google policies.

The app does not request sensitive permissions such as CALL_PHONE or READ_PHONE_STATE. The “Dial USSD” feature opens the system dialer (via ACTION_DIAL) so users can review and place calls themselves. The app does not place calls automatically.

8. Usage Disclaimer

This app is intended to help users find publicly shared USSD references for various mobile operators. We do not guarantee the validity, availability, or success of any USSD code because codes and promotions may vary by operator, SIM, region, and time. Users are responsible for verifying the terms and conditions of any service before use.
