Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 May 2024

App name: How to Draw Easter Egg

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how we handle user information in our app “How to Draw Easter Egg”. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This app is designed for general audiences and does not collect personal data from users.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as name, email, phone number, location, or device ID.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain non-personal data (such as device type, IP address, and ad interaction data) to serve relevant ads and improve performance. To ensure compliance with child protection policies, ads served in this app are configured to be suitable for general audiences.

Please refer to Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children’s Privacy

Although this app is suitable for all ages, including children, it is not specifically targeted to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data stored on our servers. Ad-related data is handled securely by Google and its partners according to their policies.

6. Permissions

This app only requires access to the Internet in order to load ads. No other sensitive permissions are requested or used.

7. Content & Image Origin

All spring-themed illustrations and drawing tutorials in this app were created using AI-generative tools. The content is original and designed for educational purposes, ensuring it is free from copyright infringement.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this document. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
